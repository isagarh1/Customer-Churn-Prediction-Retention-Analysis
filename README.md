# 📊 Customer Churn Prediction & Retention Analysis

## 🚀 Overview

Customer churn is a major revenue risk for subscription-based businesses. This project analyzes customer behavior, identifies churn drivers, and builds a predictive model to proactively flag high-risk customers.

The solution combines **data analysis, machine learning, and business intelligence** to deliver actionable insights and support retention strategies.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

* Identify customers likely to churn
* Quantify revenue at risk
* Enable targeted retention strategies

---

## 📂 Dataset

* ~6,000+ customer records
* Features include:

  * Demographics
  * Subscription details
  * Service usage
  * Billing and tenure

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

* **SQL** – Data extraction and querying
* **Python** – Data cleaning, EDA, and modeling, Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn
* **Power BI** – Dashboard development and visualization

---

## 🔍 Approach

### 1. Data Preparation

* Cleaned and transformed raw data
* Handled missing values and inconsistencies
* Performed feature selection and encoding

### 2. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

* Analyzed churn distribution and patterns
* Identified correlations between features
* Visualized customer segments and behavior

### 3. Predictive Modeling

* Built a **Random Forest classifier**
* Achieved:

  * **82% accuracy**
  * **70% recall** (focused on identifying churners)

### 4. Dashboard Development

* Designed an interactive **Power BI dashboard**
* Tracked KPIs such as:

  * Churn rate (27%)
  * Revenue at risk (~22%)
  * Customer segmentation

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Customers with **month-to-month contracts** show higher churn rates
* **Fiber-optic users** are more likely to churn
* Customers in their **first 12 months** are at highest risk
* High-value customers contribute significantly to **revenue at risk**

---

## 💡 Business Impact

* Enables early identification of high-risk customers
* Supports targeted retention campaigns
* Helps reduce revenue loss due to churn
